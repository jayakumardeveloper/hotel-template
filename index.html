OUR STORY

A Taste of Home,
Made Fresh Every Morning

At Hotel Sidambaram, every meal begins before sunrise - rice soaked, batter fermented overnight, and masalas ground fresh in-house. We serve the South Indian food you grew up loving: fluffy idlis, golden-crisp dosas, and a full banana-leaf meal with sambar, rasam, kootu, daily poriyal, appalam and home-churned curd.

100% pure vegetarian, no shortcuts, no compromises - just clean, wholesome, satisfying food in the heart of Pondicherry.
